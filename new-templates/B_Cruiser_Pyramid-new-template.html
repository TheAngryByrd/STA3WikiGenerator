Name Description
[[Reinforce Hull]] EFFECT: Increases repair rate and reduces incoming damage. Reallocate drones. Reinforce hull. Vessel has adapted defensive subroutines.
[[Shield Drain Torpedo]] EFFECT: Fires a specialised torpedo that damages enemy shields. Launch torpedo. Absorb hostile shields.
[[Assimilate Civilians]] EFFECT: Captures neutral structures. The Pyramid sends drones to assimilate and capture neutrals.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
[[Uncapturable]] Due to impenetrable security, command of this vessel cannot be taken by storm.
