==Description== Commander Donatra of the IRW Valdore ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Warbird Wing]] EFFECT: Calls a number of advanced warbirds to Donatra's aid. As the elite Commander of a wing of the Empire's most advanced warbirds, when Donatra calls for aid, it is the best who respond.
[[Righteous Cause]] As one of the top Commanders in the Romulan Star Navy, Donatra often finds herself involved in important matters of Imperial security, always ensuring that the spirit of the Empire endures. Her righteous cause inspires the crews of nearby vessels under her command, improving their tactical capabilities.
[[Thalaron Bombardment]] EFFECT: Damages enemy planets population and health during use, in addition, all structures and vessels in orbit take damage over time (friendly vessel damage is reduced by half) for 120 seconds, finally planet resource output is reduced by up to 200% at max level for ten minutes at the planet after ability use.
[[Commander Donatra]] Donatra, virtuous Commander of the Third Fleet, in command of her flagship, the first of its class, the IRW Valdore.
