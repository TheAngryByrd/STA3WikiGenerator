==Description== The medium siege cruiser modified for advanced anti-structure fire support. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Engage Siege Mode ( · )]] EFFECT: Activates Siege Mode on this individual ship. Orders this artillery cruiser to transfer power to its long-range heavy torpedo launchers, drawing power away from engines and shields in the process.
[[Engage Siege Mode (∴)]] EFFECT: Activates Siege Mode on this and all surrounding siege cruisers. Orders all nearby siege ships to transfer power to their long-range heavy torpedo launchers, drawing power away from engines and shields in the process.
[[Siege Transponder]] Designates this vessel as a siege cruiser.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
