==Description== This unique support carrier is designed to launch fighter and restore shields. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Away Team]] EFFECT: Captures neutral structures and derelict ships. An away team is beamed aboard, tasked with investigating and securing the vessel.
[[Assault Team]] A heavily armed squad is beamed aboard, attempting to capture the vessel.
[[Remote Defence Beam]] EFFECT: Replenishes the shields of surrounding friendly vessels. This command activates our vessel's Remote Defence Beam, an experimental device which restores the shield strength of allied vessels and emplacements.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
