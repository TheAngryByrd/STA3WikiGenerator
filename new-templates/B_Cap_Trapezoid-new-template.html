==Description== Designation: Vessel 3022 Vessel will seek to remove infestations and assimilate planetary targets. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Assimilate Planet]] EFFECT: Brings the planet under the Borg's control. Planet designated for assimilation. Transport assimilation drones.
[[Harvesting Device]] EFFECT: Extracts resources from the wreckage of destroyed ships and structures. Debris located. Activate harvesting device. Resources will be reallocated.
[[Siege Beam]] EFFECT: Fires a devastating beam at a planetary target. Engage siege beam. The planet must be removed of old infestations. Assimilate.
[[Collective Coordination]] Drone coordination improved. The vessel will function with increased efficiency.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
