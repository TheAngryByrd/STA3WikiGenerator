Name Description
[[Boarding Pods]] EFFECT: Causes structural damage with a chance to take over the enemy ship. If the takeover is successful, the ship is partially repaired. Engineer, launch boarding pods! Inform the strike team to commit to repairs as soon as they have control of the enemy's bridge! wa' DoS neH yIbuS, Hovmey Davan! Qapla'!
[[Righteous fervour]] EFFECT: Improves the firepower of the fleet, Long ago, the tyrant Molor was so powerful that none would stand against him. But Lord Kahless would have rather died than live under Molor's tyranny. And so, pilots, we shall defiantly stand against the enemy, and utterly defeat them in glorious battle.
[[Magnetize]] EFFECT: Magnetizes an enemy ship's hull so that passing enemy strike craft crash into the hull, causing significant damage. They'll never see this coming. Engineer, launch a magnetic charge at the enemy's hull. Their sorry excuse for fighters will assist us in bringing down their vessel!
[[Fighter Refit]] EFFECT: Outfits strike craft with additional armour and weapons at the expense of movement speed. Good! Good! More armour! Many more weapons! Our fighters must be properly equipped to annihilate our foes. For the Klingon heart always beats for war, and we always thirst for a challenge. Qapla'!
[[Engage Battle Cloak]] So'wI yIchu! In other words, saying this command renders our vessel invisible to enemy eyes, giving us a chance to strike.
