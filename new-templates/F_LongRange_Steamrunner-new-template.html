==Description== A well-armed torpedo corvette designed to engage hostile targets at long range. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Soliton Feedback Pulse]] EFFECT: Disables the enemy's impulse engines temporarily. A soliton feedback pulse is channeled through the main deflector dish of the Steamrunner-class, overloading enemy propulsion systems for a short time.
[[Proximity Torpedoes ]] EFFECT: Torpedoes are given an area of effect and longer range, at the expense of the Steamrunner's engine power. A series of high-yield proximity torpedoes are fired from the Steamrunner's weapons pod, causing large area-of-effect damage upon detonation. Note: Steamrunner must be stationary to enable proximity burst mode.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
