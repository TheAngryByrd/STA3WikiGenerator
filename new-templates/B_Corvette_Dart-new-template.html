Name Description
[[Transwarp Jump]] EFFECT: Move almost instantly to another location within the gravity well. Initiate transwarp jump. Unassimilated vessels will be identified. Study target.
[[Collective Attack]] EFFECT: Increases damage output and decreased incoming damage if multiple of this vessel type are near. Vessel coordination improved. Tactical and drone capabilities enhanced.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
[[Uncapturable]] Due to impenetrable security, command of this vessel cannot be taken by storm.
