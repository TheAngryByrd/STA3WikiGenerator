Name Description
[[Away Team]] EFFECT: Takes control of a neutral target. An away team is beamed aboard, tasked with investigating and securing the target.
[[Assault Team]] EFFECT: Takes control of a hostile target. A heavily armed squad is beamed aboard, attempting to capture the vessel.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
