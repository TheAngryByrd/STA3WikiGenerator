==Description== This fast strike cruiser is designed to provide heavy close fire support. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Anti-Proton Beam]] EFFECT: Deals direct damage and cripples the enemy's movement. This command diverts all available power to our vessel's experimental anti-proton beam, which impairs the impulse engines of enemy vessels.
[[Heavy Plasma Burn]] Due to its highly energetic and corrosive behaviour Experimental Romulan Plasma weaponry can continue to corrode a ships hull after the initial impact has been absorbed, Heavier variants of the weapon have been known to cause multiple hull breaches.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
