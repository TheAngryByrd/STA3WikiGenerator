==Description== Designation: Vessel 2219 Vessel will designate and identify new systems and species for assimilation. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Investigate]] EFFECT: Automatically scouts surrounding gravity wells. Investigate planetary systems. Return this new information to the Collective.
[[Transwarp Jump]] EFFECT: Move almost instantly to another location within the gravity well. Initiate transwarp jump. Unassimilated vessels will be identified. Study target.
[[Detect Cloak]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked ships. Initiate scan. Reveal cloaked vessels.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
[[Uncapturable]] Due to impenetrable security, command of this vessel cannot be taken by storm.
