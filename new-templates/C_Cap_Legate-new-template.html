==Description== The Legate Command Cruiser is a flexible warship that supports fleets in battle. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Stellar Shockwave]] EFFECT: Sends out a wave that damages surrounding enemy ships and lowers their accuracy. The indefatigable crew of the Legate Battleship are instructed to detonate a layer of interstellar plasma coating their secondary shield layer. The result is an impressive expanding shockwave which damages and disorients nearby ships.
[[Antimatter Siphon]] EFFECT: Sucks power from an enemy ship and adds it to the Archon's own energy reserves. The valiant crew of the Legate Command Cruiser are instructed to activate their antimatter siphon, drawing power away from an enemy vessel's warp field and transferring it to their own reserves.
[[Flow Regulators]] The valiant crew of the Legate Command Cruiser are instructed to upgrade their vessel with a pair of advanced antimatter flow regulators, ensuring optimized antimatter production and flow rate.
[[Glorious Command]] The valiant crew of the Legate Command Cruiser are committed to guiding the glorious Cardassian armada to victory. In this capacity, they will ensure that the fleet takes less damage on the whole.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
