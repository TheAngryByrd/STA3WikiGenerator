==Description== A Hybrid of Kessok and Cardassian Technology, this ship boasts the survivability of the Galor with the Damage of Kessok weapons. A Magnificent Union! ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Deflector Modulation]] EFFECT: Reflects a small portion of incoming damage back at attackers. Using valuable intelligence provided by secret operatives, the crew of this vessel are ordered to modulate their deflector frequencies to reflect incoming damage.
[[Energy Dissipation Field]] EFFECT: Temporarily increases shield mitigation of incoming damage. The crew of this vessel are ordered to augment their shields with an experimental energy dissipation field designed to heavily mitigate a portion of incoming damage.
[[Type-4A 'Revenant']] This Galor is refitted to the Type-4A 'Revenant' variant, improving damage output and speed, while reducing its armor defenses and hull strength.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
