==Description== An intimidating craft built to transport Klingon diplomats to foreign worlds. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Diplomatic Immunity]] If any ship fires upon this vessel, they, and their government, will incur the endless wrath of the Klingon Empire!
[[Establish War Academy]] EFFECT: Deploys a Klingon War Academy in this sector that allows training of Warriors within Minor Factions or establishes training facilities with major powers of the Alpha and Beta Quadrant. NOTE: You cannot deploy another embassy in the same system until your Envoy has left and returned to the system and the cooldown has expired.
[[Mutual Threat]] Special nano-bots are released from the envoy, speeding ship production and resource extraction for new relations.
[[Xeno Defense]] The envoy uses advanced defensive technology to strengthen the planet's surface from orbital bombardment.
[[Engage Battle Cloak]] So'wI yIchu! In other words, saying this command renders our vessel invisible to enemy eyes, giving us a chance to strike.
