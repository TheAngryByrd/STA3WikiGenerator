==Description== This medium beam disruptor turret will automatically fire upon any hostile vessels that pass within firing range. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Target Life Support]] This command instructs the turret to specifically target the enemy's life support systems, severely hindering their recharge and repair rates.
[[Nadion Beam Infusion]] This technique infuses nadion particles into the main flux chamber of the firing assembly, producing a supercharged disruptor beam that can incinerate even the toughest of enemy armour plating.
