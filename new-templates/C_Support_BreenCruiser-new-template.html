==Description== A deadly strike cruiser that can disable vessels with an energy-draining shot. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Energy Drain Torpedo]] EFFECT: Fires a weapon that deals significant damage but also devastates the target's shield mitigation. The crew of this vessel are ordered to fire a deadly Energy Drain Torpedo, sapping the power of targeted ships, while also disabling their propulsion systems, weapons, and shields, making them easy prey for the rest of the fleet.
[[Anti-Cloak Scan]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked enemies. The crew of this vessel are ordered to activate a set of advanced anti-cloak scanning sensors, revealing cloaked mines and hostile vessels in the vicinity. Due to the power of these sensors, all systems will be disabled during the scan.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
