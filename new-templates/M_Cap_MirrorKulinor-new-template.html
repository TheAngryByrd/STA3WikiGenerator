==Description== The Kulinor is a strong vessel equipped with several exotic, stolen technologies. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Micro Torpedo Burst]] EFFECT: Fires weapons in all directions, devastating surrounding strike craft. The crew of this vessel are commanded to fire a rapid burst of micro photon torpedoes set for proximity detonation. This produces a formidable flak burst effect that damages enemy fighters.
[[Viral Matrix]] EFFECT: Deals significant damage and temporarily disables many of a target ship's systems, with a chance of assimilation. Activate Viral Matrix. Matrix will infect nearby vessels. They will be assimilated.
[[Adapt Armor]] Adapt. Tactical armor has been adapted. Damage reduction enhanced by 3% per target engaged. We have adapted.
[[Inspiring Presence]] EFFECT: Increases the amount of damage Federation shields can absorb 1/2/3/4 percent and how quickly they restore.The fleet is inspired by the presence of the crew of the Enterprise, driving them to perform to the best of their ability.
[[Uncapturable]] Due to impenetrable security, command of this vessel cannot be taken by storm.
