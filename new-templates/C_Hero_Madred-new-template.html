==Description== A well respected Union Guard officer. How many lights do you see? ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Sadistic Tendancies]] EFFECT: Grants Madreds vessel improved Ability Cooldown Rate, Fire Rate and Improves Chance to Hit. Madred's sadistic thoughts and actions have taught him a thing or two about how to deal with threats. His many years of experience in interrogation and mental control help him to break down enemies on a psychological level and defeat them.
[[Metagenic Weapon]] EFFECT: Damages enemy planets population and health during use, in addition all structures and vessels in orbit take damage over time (friendly vessel damage is reduced by half) for 120 seconds, finally ship construction speed is reduced by up to 200% at max level for ten minutes at the planet after ability use. Gul Madred has learned that there is no line to cross. He readily employs biogenic weaponry against any living being, even his own troops.
[[Fear Factor]] EFFECT: Increases Armour, Damage Reduction and Hull Restore rates in a 6000km radius of Madred. The fear that Gul Madred instills in his troops forces them to operate at peak efficiency, leading to highly impressive defensive maneuvers and repair times for his vessel. No one would dare to disappoint him, or else 'the lights'.
[[Gul Madred]] Gul Madred, a highly respected and feared Union Guard interrogation officer. How many lights do you see?
