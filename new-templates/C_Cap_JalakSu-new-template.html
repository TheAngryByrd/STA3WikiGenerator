==Description== Jalak-Su class Son'a Battlecruiser ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Torpedo Volley]] EFFECT: Fires a volley of torpedoes that can strike multiple targets. After the initial incident with the Enterprise-E the Son'a were also able to develop their own variety of Quantum Torpedo's and fire them in wide arcs.
[[Isolytic Burst]] EFFECT: Creates an area that will temporarily disable, damage and drain antimatter of any enemy ship within it. This subspace weapon opens a Subspace Tear that is detrimental to the warp cores of nearby ships and therefore their Antimatter supplies.
[[Phasing Particle Beam]] EFFECT: Damages a target and dampens its weapon systems. A deadly particle beam that binds its target with a phased particle bombardment penetrating shields and damaging enemy weapon points as they return fire.
[[Dark Predator]] EFFECT: Boosts shield and weapon systems at the expense of antimatter and passive regeneration. The Jalak-Su drives fear into many, known for its dark metallic hull and bladed edges, this often has caused Son'a crews to act more boldly in combat, sometimes to their detriment.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
