==Description== An experimental temporal warship built to lead fearlessly from the front lines. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Quantum Torp. Volley]] EFFECT: Fires a spread of quantum torpedoes at surrounding enemies. In an effort to break the enemy's resolve, the captain of the Polaris orders a full barrage of Quantum Torpedoes to be fired, striking multiple hostile targets simultaneously.
[[Multi-Adaptive Shields]] EFFECT: Converts a percentage of incoming weapons energy into the Polaris' weapons and shields. Utilizing advanced multi-adaptive shield technology, the Polaris is able to absorb incoming weapons fire and divert it to reinforce shields and weapons systems.
[[Gemini Assault]] EFFECT: Brings in a second Polaris from a parallel universe to temporarily fight by this Polaris' side. Newly approved by Starfleet Command, the Gemini Matrix is a powerful device which summons doppelgangers of the host vessel from a near-identical parallel universe. Once activated, all vessels will be prepared for a temporal assault run.
[[Temporal Shockwave]] EFFECT: Freezes time for surrounding enemy ships but disables the weapons on ALL surrounding ships (both friendly and enemy) except for the Polaris herself. A direct result of the many experiments conducted at the Manheim Temporal Research Institute, the Polaris has been equipped with an experimental Manheim Wave Generator designed to suddenly freeze the temporal reference frame in the surrounding gravity well, while still allowing the Polaris the chance to attack.
