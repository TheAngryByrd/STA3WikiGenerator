==Description== A fast and maneuverable battleship equipped with advanced weaponry. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Full Barrage]] EFFECT: Vastly improves fire rate at the expense of speed and maneuverability. This command diverts all power to the main disruptor banks, significantly increasing fire rate at the expense of shields and propulsion systems.
[[Combat Maneuver]] EFFECT: Greatly improves speed, maneuverability and evasion. This command establishes a synergistic link between our helmsmen and the tactical computer, markedly improving our maneuverability and speed.
[[Quantum Ballista]] EFFECT: Fires a highly damaging projectile at a target. This command channels energy from the quantum singularity generator into a single high-yield projectile, which has a chance to strike other ships in its path.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
