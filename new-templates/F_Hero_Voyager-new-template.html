==Description== Kathryn Janeway, Captain of the USS Voyager NCC-74656. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Launch Delta Flyer]] EFFECT: Launches the Delta Flyer to support Voyager, capable of deploying a holographic fleet (Taunts Targets), and target weapons array (35% energy weapon cooldown) and equipped with point defence (25% damage reduction).
[[Launch Type-10 Probe]] EFFECT: Deploys a type 10 probe to a chosen gravity well anywhere on the map that shows vision of the well and affects both trade and culture rates at the target 15/20/25/30%. Voyager was equipped with an assortment of probes as an exploration vessel, Type 10 probes provide intelligence data while creating sensor shadows to disrupt enemy sublight trade communications and culture broadcasts.
