==Description== Deploys a powerful turret to provide heavy additional fire support for the Hutet. The glorious and honourable crew of the mighty Hutet Dreadnought have also ensured that their proud vessel is able to defend itself. In this capacity, a large C-312 defence Platform will deploy from the underside of the vessel and destroy any hostile vessel in range of the Hutet. ==Abilities==
