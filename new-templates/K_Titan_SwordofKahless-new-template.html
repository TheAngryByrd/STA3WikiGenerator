==Description== A mighty dreadnought worthy of the Lord Kahless himself. For the Empire! ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Shockwave Torpedo]] EFFECT: Fires a torpedo that emits a shockwave that flings enemy ships in all directions, dealing heavy damage and ruining enemy formations. Gunner, prepare a Shockwave Torpedo. We will smite their pitiful ship, and blast apart any pathetic escort ships that they have nearby. Hah! They suspect nothing.
[[Overwhelming Assault]] EFFECT: Dramatically increases weapon performance, especially if Bastion Shield is activated. Warriors! Let none stand in our path! We are relentless! Furious! Victorious! For the Empire! All batteries, fire at will!
[[Bastion Shield]] EFFECT: Drastically improves forward shields. Engineer, activate Bastion Shield! Their pitiful frontal assaults will not be able to penetrate our forward shields. And we just might have a surprise in store for them...heheheh. The poor fools. Qapla'!
[[Stormfury Torpedo]] EFFECT: Fires a torpedo that deals cumulative damage and also drains the target's antimatter reserves. Gunner, prepare a Stormfury Torpedo. When this strikes...their vessels will be crippled, shields and antimatter will be reduced, and we will close in for the kill.
[[Engage Battle Cloak]] So'wI yIchu! In other words, saying this command renders our vessel invisible to enemy eyes, giving us a chance to strike.
