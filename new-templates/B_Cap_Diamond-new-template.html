==Description== Designation: Vessel 1889 Vessel will seek to intercept hostile vessels and prepare them for assimilation. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Antimatter Recharge]] EFFECT: Replenishes the energy reserves of a nearby friendly ship. Engage transfer beam. The Collective's antimatter storage will be replenished.
[[Adapt Hull Matrix]] EFFECT: Gives a massive boost to repair rate and armour strength, effectively making an allied frigate or cruiser all but invulnerable for a time. Adapt. Reinforce hull of target vessel. Our vessel will be impervious to attack.
[[Ultritium Burst]] EFFECT: Fires a beam that arcs between multiple enemy ships, doing significant cumulative damage. Dispel Ultritium particles. Ultritium pulses will arc to nearby vessels. Hostile vessels will be ready for assimilation.
[[Nanite Projector]] EFFECT: Increases the amount of damage an enemy unit takes, and lowers their accuracy for a time. Project disruption nanites. Infect communications array. Hostile user interface systems will malfunction.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
