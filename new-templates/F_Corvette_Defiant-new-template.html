==Description== A tough little warship equipped with advanced weaponry and a minelayer. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Multi-Layered Shielding]] EFFECT: Significantly strengthens shields for a period of time. Overlapping shield layers offer superior damage mitigation to standard shields found on other Federation vessels of this size.
[[Agile Frame]] The Defiant was built to withstand extreme inertial displacement and can maneuver better than most federation starships making it hard for enemies to track.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
