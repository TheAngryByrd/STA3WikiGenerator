==Description== Hailing from the mirror universe, the ISS Imperator is the embodiment of the resurgent Terran Empire's power! ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Ion Cannon]] EFFECT: Fires an enormously destructive energy beam at a single target. Gunner, activate Ion Cannon! We have only yet begun to show the enemy the full might of the Klingon Empire! May they all burn in the fires of Gre'thor!
[[Target Weapons Array]] EFFECT: Renders the enemy unable to fire for a short time. Upgrades to the tactical sensors on the Miranda-class allow it to specifically target and cripple enemy weapons systems.
[[Fire At Will]] EFFECT: Dramatically increases firing rate and coverage at the expense of firepower. Commands the tactical officers to fire all available weapons at will. This increases the quantity and coverage of weapons fire but will reduce the damage per shot.
[[Corbomite Reflector]] EFFECT: Returns a portion of incoming weapons damage to the attacker. Generates a powerful Corbomite Shield, reflecting a portion of hostile weapons fire back toward the attacker's vessel.
[[Uncapturable]] Due to impenetrable security, command of this vessel cannot be taken by storm.
