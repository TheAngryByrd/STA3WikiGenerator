==Description== The Keldon-4B is a powerful and heavily armored battleship based on the Galor Class Cruiser. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Engineering Drones]] EFFECT: Repairs surrounding friendly vessels. The cruiser's valiant crew are instructed to use their large contingent of specialized repair drones to assist allied vessels during combat.
[[Armored Warfare]] The Keldon coordinates the fleet, tightening its formation and ensuring that it minimizes the exposure of weak-points
[[Point Defense System]] EFFECT: Reduces incoming damage. The indefatigable crew of this ship are instructed to activate their advanced point defense systems to shoot down inbound weapons fire, reducing incoming damage by a significant margin.
[[Sensor Scrambler]] The indefatigable crew of the Keldon Battleship are instructed to use their communications systems to scramble enemy sensors with amplified noise, reducing their ability achieve target lock.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
