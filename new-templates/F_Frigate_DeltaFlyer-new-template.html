==Description== An older vessel repurposed to assist the fleet in eliminating enemy strike craft. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Tactical Holofleet]] EFFECT: Generates a number of fake ships around the Delta Flyer to draw the enemy's fire. Using technology developed in the Delta Quadrant, the Delta Flyer is now able to temporarily deploy a small fleet of holographic ships to be used as decoys.
[[Target Weapons Array]] EFFECT: Renders an enemy temporarily unable to fire. The Delta Flyer's Borg enhanced tactical suite enables it to specifically target and cripple enemy weapons systems.
[[Point Defence System]] EFFECT: Reduces incoming damage. Advanced point defence systems installed on-board are activated, intercepting a significant portion of weapons fire.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
