==Description== The C-313 is the latest invention from the peerless S.G. Dukat Labs on Cardassia Prime. Owing to its illustrious origins, it is, without question, the most sophisticated defense platform in the Alpha Quadrant. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Threat Detection]] All C-313 defense platforms in the local vicinity are set up to share sensor data and coordinate their attacks on invading ships, improving their combat efficiency.
[[Sensor Dampening Field]] A series of powerful sensor dampeners installed on-board this C-313 prevents hostile warships from attaining reliable tactical sensor locks on allied vessels.
