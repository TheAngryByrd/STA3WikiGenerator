==Description== A small, nimble scout vessel designed to gather intelligence about our enemies. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Surveillance]] EFFECT: Automatically scouts surrounding gravity wells. The crew of this vessel are ordered to gather information about the sector.
[[Micro Anti-Cloak Scan]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked enemies. The crew of this vessel are ordered to briefly scan for cloaked vessels in range.
[[Minesweeper]] The crew of this vessel are ordered to clear mines for the rest of the fleet.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
