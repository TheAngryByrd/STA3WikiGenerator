==Description== A mysterious battlecruiser equipped with experimental support technologies. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Holographic Fleet]] EFFECT: Generates the image of several cruisers around the Phoenix that draw the enemy's fire. This command activates our vessel's experimental holographic projectors, fabricating a fleet of decoy ships that tricks the enemy into firing at mirages.
[[Psionic Blast]] EFFECT: Significantly reduces the target's accuracy. This command activates our vessel's experimental Psionic Field Generator, which bypasses shields and sends the enemy fleet's officers into a paranoid state, affecting their targeting ability.
[[Remote Defence Field]] EFFECT: Reinforces the shields of surrounding friendly vessels. This command activates a scaled-up version of the Dominus-class's Remote defence Beam, which reinforces the shields of all allied vessels within range.
[[Singularity Projector]] EFFECT: Deploys a small black hole that can wreak havoc on enemy formations and cause heavy damage. This command activates our vessel's Quantum Singularity Projector, a device capable of remotely generating a small black hole which traps and damages targeted vessels for a short time.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
