==Description== An older command cruiser designed to settle and defend the outlying colonies. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Colonize]] Transports supplies and personnel down to the surface of the planet to establish a new Federation colony.
[[Target Impulse Coils]] EFFECT: Disables enemy capital ships and dreadnoughts engines for an extended duration
[[Antimatter Shunt]] EFFECT: Replenishes antimatter stores on surrounding allied ships. The Ambassador, in an attempt to support other Federation vessels activates a resonance wave that greatly improves antimatter reaction rates in Federation warp cores.
[[Long Range Scan]] EFFECT: Briefly grants a live feed of a distant gravity well. Diverts available power to long-range sensors, allowing us to observe distant systems with a series of high-resolution optical and subspace sensor scans.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
