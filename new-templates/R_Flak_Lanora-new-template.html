==Description== This small, but fast anti-fighter escort is designed to eliminate enemy strike craft. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Tachyon Burst]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked enemy units. This command unleashes a powerful tachyon burst that reveals cloaked enemy vessels up to 9500 units away.
[[Defensive Fire]] Defensive fire erupts from the vessel, destroying small craft nearby with ease.
[[Covert Evasives]] EFFECT: Increases manoeuvrability for a time. Evasive manoeuvres active. We shall slip between their midst like shadows in the night.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
