==Description== An impressive war destroyer with the capability to restore shield defenses. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Away Team]] EFFECT: Captures neutral structures and derelict ships. An away team is beamed aboard, tasked with investigating and securing the vessel.
[[Assault Team]] EFFECT: Captures a damaged enemy vessel. A heavily armed squad is beamed aboard, attempting to capture the vessel.
[[Emittive Shield Restore]] EFFECT: Fires a beam that restores the shields of a targeted friendly vessel. The crew of this vessel are commanded to launch a shield restore beam at a slight energy cost to their own shield strength, assisting a single allied vessel in need.
[[Anti-Cloak Scan]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked enemies. The crew of this vessel are ordered to activate a set of advanced anti-cloak scanning sensors, revealing cloaked mines and hostile vessels in the vicinity. Due to the power of these sensors, all systems will be disabled during the scan.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
