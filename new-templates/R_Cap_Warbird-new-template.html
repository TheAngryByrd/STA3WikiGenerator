==Description== An intimidating battleship built to project the power of the Romulan Star Empire. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Absorption Layer]] EFFECT: Bolsters this vessels shields. This command temporarily reinforces our vessel's shield matrix with a resilient quantum absorption layer, helping to further resist incoming weapons fire.
[[Shield Inversion Beam]] EFFECT: Drains a portion of a targets shields and applies it to this vessel. This command activates the experimental Shield Inversion Beam, a devious weapon that restores our vessel's shield strength at the notable expense of an opponent's.
[[Gravitic Shockwave]] EFFECT: Causes damage over time and decreases enemy accuracy while temporarily leaving the Warbird stationary. This command initiates a perturbation within our vessel's quantum singularity field, generating an expanding gravitic shockwave notably designed to damage the hull integrity and targeting systems of all enemy vessels within its path.
[[Cloak Fleet]] EFFECT: Renders all friendly ships in the area temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command orders all Imperial Romulan Warbirds within range to engage their cloaking devices, hiding themselves from enemy sensors.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
