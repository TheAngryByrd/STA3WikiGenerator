==Description== A massive Explorer-type battleship equipped with outstanding firepower. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Photon Torpedo Volley]] EFFECT: Fires a split of torpedoes that strike multiple surrounding targets. The Galaxy-class unleashes a full volley of photon torpedoes at the enemy, striking multiple hostile targets simultaneously.
[[Tachyon Beam]] EFFECT: Fires a powerful energy beam at an enemy target. Fires an inverted tachyon beam from the Galaxy's main deflector dish that quickly drains the target's shields over time.
[[Saucer Separation]] EFFECT: Temporarily separates the Galaxy-class into two independent vessels. In emergency situations, Galaxy-class starships are equipped to separate the Saucer section from the faster Stardrive section in order to combat the enemy.
[[Antimatter Spread]] EFFECT: Slows the firing rate of surrounding enemies and decreases their accuracy. Initiates the Antimatter Spread, which uses a timed antimatter burst as a pyrotechnical display around the ship, effectively blinding hostiles for a time.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
