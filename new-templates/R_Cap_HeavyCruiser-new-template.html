==Description== A powerful carrier built to overwhelm comparable vessels with strike craft. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Efficient Hangars]] EFFECT: Allows an extra squadron of strike craft to be built. This upgrade incites our flight deck crews to overhaul the hangar bays to store additional squadrons of fighters.
[[Myotronic Beam]] EFFECT: Degrades enemy weapon systems. This command activates the Myotronic Beam, a weapon reverse-engineered from salvaged Dominion technology that heavily disrupts enemy weapons systems for a short period of time.
[[Plasma Implosion]] EFFECT: Damages strike craft and small starships. This command initiates an expanding drive plasma implosion that will heavily damage fighters and corvettes, and will also affect enemy targeting sensors.
[[Advanced Strike Craft]] EFFECT: Enhances this vessel's strike craft. This upgrade, promoted by our vessel's flight deck engineers, improves the agility and firepower of our strike craft with a number of state-of-the-art modifications.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
