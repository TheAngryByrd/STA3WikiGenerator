==Description== This mighty cruiser is designed to defeat other Cruisers in direct combat. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Plasma Barrage]] EFFECT: Fires a barrage that causes direct damage and weakens enemy armor for a time. This command overloads the primary plasma torpedo assembly, unleashing a violent, conical blast of superheated plasma bolts at the enemy fleet.
[[Tachyon Emitter]] EFFECT: Detects surrounding cloaked vessels. This emitter utilizes pulsed tachyon emissions in order to detect cloaked vessels.
[[Heavy Plasma Burn]] Due to its highly energetic and corrosive behavior Experimental Romulan Plasma weaponry can continue to corrode a ships hull after the initial impact has been absorbed, Heavier variants of the weapon have been known to cause multiple hull breachs
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
