==Description== A massive Space Control Ship built to lead Federation fleets in times of war. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Strategic Command]] EFFECT: Reduces incoming damage for surrounding friendly ships. As the Federation's Space Control Ship, the Excalibur is tasked with coordinating various strategic operations, mitigating incoming damage whenever possible.
[[United Defenses]] EFFECT: Strengthens and replenishes the shields of surrounding friendly ships. In an attempt to rescue the fleet, the Excalibur transfers shield energy to its nearby allies, helping to restore their defenses at a vastly improved rate.
[[Verteron Generator]] EFFECT: Greatly inhibits the ability of surrounding enemy ships to use their warp drives. In order to prevent the enemy fleet from escaping, the Excalibur's verteron field generator is activated, destabilizing enemy warp fields for a short time.
[[For The Federation!]] EFFECT: Repairs hull damage and slightly improves the weapons systems of surrounding friendly ships. With the survival of the Federation on the line, a fiery speech is delivered by the captain of the Excalibur, inspiring the fleet to enter their finest hour.
