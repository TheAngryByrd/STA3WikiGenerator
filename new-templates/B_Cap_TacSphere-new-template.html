==Description== Designation: Vessel 2001. Long-range tactical vessel. Eliminates threats to expedite rapid assimilation. ==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Nanite Consumption]] EFFECT: Disables all engines on an enemy vessel temporarily and causes cumulative damage. Release nanites. Acquire and process derelict vessel components. Return processed components to the Collective.
[[Feedback Pulse]] EFFECT: While active, redirects a portion of incoming damage to the attacker. Adapt. Redirect energy from incoming attack. Reroute through tactical grid. Modulate. Emit Feedback Pulse.
[[Absorb Fire]] Adapt to incoming fire. Divert absorbed energy to antimatter reserve nodes.
[[Obliterate]] EFFECT: Enhances the weapons of surrounding friendly vessels, causing additional damage to enemy armour and weapons. Enhance tactical systems. Hostile species vessels will be prepared for assimilation.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
