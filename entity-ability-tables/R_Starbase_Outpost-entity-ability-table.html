==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Outpost Defense]] The outpost command center can control nearby turret installations to optimize their fire patterns and range
[[Holographic Fleet]] EFFECT: Generates the image of several cruisers around the Phoenix that draw the enemy's fire. This command activates our vessel's experimental holographic projectors, fabricating a fleet of decoy ships that tricks the enemy into firing at mirages.
