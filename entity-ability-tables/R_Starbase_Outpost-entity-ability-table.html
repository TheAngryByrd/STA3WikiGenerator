==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Engineering Teams]] Engineering teams on-board the spacedock begin repairs on nearby allied vessels.
[[Outpost Defence]] The outpost command centre can control nearby turret installations to optimize their fire patterns and range.
[[Quantum Absorption]] This upgrade installs a starbase-sized version of the Quantum Absorption experimental generator on-board the station, bolstering its shield defences.
[[Merchant Facilities]] This upgrade allows for a limited number of authorized foreign vendors to sell their imported goods at the station's bazaar.
