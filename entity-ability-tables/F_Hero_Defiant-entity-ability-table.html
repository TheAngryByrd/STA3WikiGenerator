==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Emissary's Calling]] EFFECT: Grants increased fire rate, damage and chance to hit in a small radius of Sisko. Captain Sisko's innate battle prowess and steady leadership inspires the fleet to fight at peak efficiency, increasing the effectiveness of their weapons systems.
[[Pocket Battleship]] EFFECT: Grants increased angular and linear speed as well as a chance to evade incoming fire, further improvements to weapons for the defiant including rate of fire and ability to hit evasive targets. With O'Brien's handiwork the USS Defiant is faster, more manoeuvrable and more deadly than even ships of the same formidable class.
[[Forced Evac]] EFFECT: Removes a percentage of population and applies a 10 minute slow population growth rate modifier, Sisko broadcasts fake sensor data to the planet forcing them to Evacuate for concerns over an impending disaster.
[[USS Defiant]] USS Defiant NX-74205 under the command of Captain Benjamin Sisko.
