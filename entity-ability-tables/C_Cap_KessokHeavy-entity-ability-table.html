==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Passive Decoy System]] EFFECT: Makes enemies more likely to target this ship. The electronic warfare systems on the Kessok Heavy assist other vessels in the fleet from being targeted by focusing those targeting systems back on itself.
[[Repair Binding]] EFFECT: Repairs friendly ships and stations. Heavy Kessok Vessels are equipped with powerful siliconite repair beams capable of replacing damaged hull with a siliconite coating.
[[Siliconite Shielding]] EFFECT: Reduces incoming damage. The Kessok are able to manipulate their hull but also their shields, the more their vessels take damage the more they are able to absorb, it is still possible however to overwhelm their shield grids eventually with sustained fire.
[[Target Weapons]] EFFECT: Decreases the enemy's accuracy. The Kessok can directly target enemy weapon points with accurate beam fire; often disabling them.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
