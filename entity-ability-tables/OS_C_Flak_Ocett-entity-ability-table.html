==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Light Anti-Cloak Scan]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked enemies. The crew of this vessel are ordered to activate their short-range anti-cloak scan, revealing cloaked mines and hostile vessels in the local vicinity.
[[Micro Torpedo Burst]] EFFECT: Fires weapons in all directions, devastating surrounding strike craft. The crew of this vessel are commanded to fire a rapid burst of micro photon torpedoes set for proximity detonation. This produces a formidable flak burst effect that damages enemy fighters.
[[Defensive Flak]] Defensive fire erupts from the vessel, destroying small craft nearby with ease.
