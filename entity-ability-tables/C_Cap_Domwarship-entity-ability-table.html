==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Heavy Polaron Torp.]] EFFECT: Fires a single torpedo that does heavy damage and temporarily disrupts an enemy's shields. The unparalleled crew of the Mantis Heavy Battlecruiser are instructed to fire a single Heavy Polaron Torpedo, a weapon which both inflicts serious damage and disrupts the normal operation of critical systems.
[[Gamma Assault]] EFFECT: Boosts weapon damage and turn rate. The unparalleled crew of the Mantis Heavy Battlecruiser are instructed to initiate the Gamma Assault Pattern, a combat preset that diverts power to weapons and manoeuvring thrusters, allowing our vessel to bring its weapons to bear and blast our enemy to oblivion.
[[Polaric Resonance]] The unparalleled crew of the Mantis Heavy Battlecruiser are instructed to constantly rotate their weapons' polaric frequencies, inflicting additional hull damage due to resonance bursts that ripple through enemy's shield defences.
[[Vorta Interfacing]] The unparalleled Vorta commanders of the Mantis Heavy Battlecruisers are trained to masterfully interface with the fleet's propulsion systems, leading to an increase in speed. Note: this ability can stack twice before it is fully capped.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
