==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Diplomatic Immunity]] Broadcasts of peace prevent the envoy from being targeted for a short time.
[[Establish Spy Post]] EFFECT: Deploys a Tal Shiar Spy Outpost in this sector that allows negotiation with Minor Factions or Allows manipulation of local government allowing improved relations with major powers of the Alpha and Beta Quadrant. NOTE: You cannot deploy another embassy in the same system until your Envoy has left and returned to the system and the cooldown has expired.
[[Mutual Threat]] Special nano-bots are released from the envoy, speeding ship production and resource extraction for new relations.
[[Xeno Defence]] The envoy uses advanced defensive technology to strengthen the planet's surface from orbital bombardment.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
