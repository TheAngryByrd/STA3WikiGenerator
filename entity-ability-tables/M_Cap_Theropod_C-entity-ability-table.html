==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Graviton Flux Field]] EFFECT: Degrades surrounding enemy weapons systems and engines. This command activates our vessel's miniature Graviton Flux Generator, a top-secret device designed to interfere with the targeting systems and sublight engines of enemy vessels within range.
[[Engineering Teams]] EFFECT: Repair the hulls and replenish the shields of friendly vessels. Engineering teams stationed aboard Phoenix-class vessels are very hastily beamed over to nearby ships, helping to seal hull breaches and repair damaged subsystems in the midst of battle.
[[Singularity Projector]] EFFECT: Deploys a small black hole that can wreak havoc on enemy formations and cause heavy damage. This command activates our vessel's Quantum Singularity Projector, a device capable of remotely generating a small black hole which traps and damages targeted vessels for a short time.
[[Uncapturable]] Due to impenetrable security, command of this vessel cannot be taken by storm.
