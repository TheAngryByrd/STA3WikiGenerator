==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Tactical Analysis ]] EFFECT: Allows allied units to do more damage to a target. The Intrepid-class initiates an extensive tactical analysis of a particular attacker, highlighting weak points in their defenses, and transmitting the findings to the fleet.
[[Tachyon Pulse Beacon]] EFFECT: Sends out a pulse that reveals surrounding cloaked units. Activates a tachyon pulse beacon from the main deflector that releases a particulate burst every ten seconds that will detect cloaked vessels up to 24000 units away.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
