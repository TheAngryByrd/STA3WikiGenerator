==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Elite Saboteurs]] EFFECT: Damages an enemy ship, with a chance of chaining the effect to other ships. The resourceful crew of the Archon Battle Carrier are instructed to beam over a team of ex-Obsidian Order saboteurs, who will proceed to plant bombs in critical locations throughout the target ship. When they have completed their mission, the saboteurs will attempt to beam over to additional enemy ships and sabotage their systems as well.
[[Magnetic Subversion]] EFFECT: Magnetizes the hull of an enemy ship, causing enemy strike craft to slam into it and cause damage. The resourceful crew of the Archon Battle Carrier are instructed to launch a powerful electro-magnetic charge at a single enemy vessel, attracting nearby enemy strike craft and causing them to slam into the unfortunate vessel's hull.
[[Lockdown Mode]] EFFECT: Greatly increases weapon performance at the expense of mobility. The resourceful crew of the Archon Battle Carrier are instructed to activate 'Lockdown Mode', drawing power from propulsion systems and diverting them to bolster tactical and defence systems.
[[Hangar Expansion]] The resourceful crew of the Archon Battle Carrier are instructed to optimize the storage space of their hanger bays to accommodate additional strike craft.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
