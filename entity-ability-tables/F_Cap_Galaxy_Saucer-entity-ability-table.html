==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Reintegration]] Initiates reintegration of the vessel.
[[Target Weapons Array]] The Galaxy-class Saucer section is fitted with powerful phaser arrays, so powerful they can damage enemy weapon systems in a single blast.
[[Phaser Overload]] The Galaxy-class Saucer section can overcharge its phasers however while doing so it can cause the saucer section to be susceptible to further damage itself.
[[Antimatter Spread]] EFFECT: Slows the firing rate of surrounding enemies and decreases their accuracy. Initiates the Antimatter Spread, which uses a timed antimatter burst as a pyrotechnical display around the ship, effectively blinding hostiles for a time.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
