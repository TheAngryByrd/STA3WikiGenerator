==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Deploy Monac Stardock]] The crew of this vessel are commanded to establish the mighty Monac Stardock, the Dominion's most powerful starbase. A massive fortification, the Monac possesses incredible firepower and is found at key strategic sites throughout the Dominion.
[[Deploy Evas Starbase]] The crew of this vessel are commanded to establish the venerable Evas Stardock, Cardassia's most powerful starbase. A massive fortification, the Evas possesses incredible defenses and is found at key strategic sites throughout the Union.
