==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Target Weapons Array]] EFFECT: Renders an enemy temporarily unable to fire. Veteran tactical officers can specifically target and cripple enemy weapons systems.
[[Expert Evasion]] EFFECT: Temporarily makes the ship faster and more likely to evade incoming fire. The helmsmen of older starships don't let the limitations of their ships limit them. Utilising their resources, extensive experience and some good old-fashioned luck these ships handle more like smaller newer generations of starships.
