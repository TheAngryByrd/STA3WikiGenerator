==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Reintegration]] Initiates reintegration of the vessel.
[[Heavy Torpedo Salvo]] The Galaxy-class engineering section, when in battle mode can unleash powerful torpedo salvos when separated from the saucer section.
[[Unbound Manoeuvres]] Unencumbered by the massive Saucer section, the Galaxy-class Stardrive section can devote its excess energy to significant feats of speed and manoeuvring.
[[Braced Shielding]] Shield power that normally sustains a defensive bubble around the entirety of the Galaxy-class can now be retracted to encompass the much more compact engineering section. This results in greater shield durability and recharge rate while detached from the Saucer section.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
