==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Nanite Repair Beams]] Commence maintenance operations.
[[Shield Drain Torpedo]] EFFECT: Fires a specialised torpedo that damages enemy shields. Launch torpedo. Absorb hostile shields.
[[Holding Beam]] EFFECT: Locks on to an enemy ship and holds it in place. If frigates or cruisers take sufficient damage while held, they will be assimilated instead of destroyed. Activate Holding Beam. Transport drones. They will be assimilated.
[[Assimilation Cloud]] EFFECT: Sends out a pulse that disables enemy abilities and has a chance of assimilating nearby enemy ships. Release combat nanoprobes. The carbon-units will be assimilated.
