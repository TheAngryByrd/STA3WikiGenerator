==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Diplomatic Immunity]] Broadcasts of peace prevent the envoy from being targeted for a short time.
[[Establish Embassy]] EFFECT: Deploys a Federation Embassy in this sector that allows negotiation with Minor Factions or sends diplomats to improve relations with major powers of the Alpha and Beta Quadrant through trade. NOTE: You cannot deploy another embassy in the same system until your Envoy has left and returned to the system and the cooldown has expired.
[[Diplomatic Functions]] 'Have we not heard the chimes at midnight?' Lavish banquets and pleasant company have made parting such sweet, sweet sorrow.
[[Arbitrate Tariff]] Special trade policies are enforced by the envoy, boosting production income and developing improved relations.
[[Settlers]] An engineering team assists in the development of new metropolitan sectors, increasing the population.
