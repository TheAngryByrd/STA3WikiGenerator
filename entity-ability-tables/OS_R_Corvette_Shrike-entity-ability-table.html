==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Shield Formation]] EFFECT: Increases chance to hit and shield restore rate at the limited expense of evasion if multiple of this vessel type are near. Adept at commanding stealthy raids into the neutral zone, Romulan corvette captains learned long ago that overlapping shield projection could ensure they made it back home.
[[Plasma Burn]] EFFECT: Plasma weapons cause damage over time. Due to its highly energetic and corrosive behaviour Experimental Romulan Plasma weaponry can continue to corrode a ships hull after the initial impact has been absorbed.
[[Activate Cloak]] EFFECT: Renders this vessel temporarily invisible and undetectable. This command activates our vessel's cloaking device, rendering it invisible to all conventional EM sensor bands.
