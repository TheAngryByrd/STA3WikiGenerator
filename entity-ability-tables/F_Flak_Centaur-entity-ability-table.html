==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Tachyon Emission]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked units. Activates a tachyon emission from the deflector array to detect cloaked vessels up to a range of 9500 units away.
[[Flak Defense]] The Centaur's multi-tracker point defense phaser arrays allow them to cover the fleet from fighter and bomber attacks.
[[Evasive Maneuvers]] EFFECT: Boosts speed and maneuverability, and increases chance to dodge incoming fire. Masterful piloting by specially-trained helmsmen allows Saber and Centaur Destroyer vessels to easily dodge enemy weapons fire while engaging targets at high impulse.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
