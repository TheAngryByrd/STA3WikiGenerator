==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Singularity Jump]] EFFECT: Instantly Traverse via singularity 20,000KM up to 50,000KM around the gravity well while simultaneously granting armour and antimatter absorption. Gul Kentar's obsession with Species 8472 and their Singularity technology has resulted in the development of a new armour material, Kentar's Vessel is the prototype, the material properties enhance defences and draws antimatter from energy attacks after it absorbs fluidic space particles while allowing traversal of fluidic space.
[[Summon Species 8472]] EFFECT: Opens a rift and summons a fully functional Species 8472 Bioship. Unlike other vessels of its class, Kentars vessel has been able to lure the strongest of all species 8472 bio organisms. Cardassian ships are seen as biotic due to isotonic particles infused into the shields which makes Species 8472 bioships detect them as friendly. The summoned bioships will only attack for short time before their pilots order their vessels to self destruct.
[[C-305K Siege Support]] EFFECT: Deploys a turret to bombard a targeted planet dealing from 105 at level 1 to 175 damage at level 4 every 4 seconds with higher levels, also this platform can be deployed up to 17500km away from the planet. The impassioned Gul Kentar orders the deployment of a heavily-modified siege turret designed to assist in the complete annihilation of non-Cardassian colonies.
[[Gul Kentar]] Gul Kentar, Leader of the Third Order, trans-dimensional researcher, and outspoken proponent of Cardassian isolationism. Don't get on his bad side.
