==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Isolytic Mines]] EFFECT: Drops mines that can be manually detonated in the vicinity of enemies in range to cause heavy damage and limit their ability to move. Deploy special Isolytic Mines that drain antimatter from the target and leave them crippled.
[[Vent Metreon Gas]] EFFECT: Creates an area that will damage and slow enemy ship within it. The Son'a store the normally unstable Metreon Gas, abundant in their home region of space, and use it's deadly nature against enemy ships that have not been adapted to tolerate it.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
