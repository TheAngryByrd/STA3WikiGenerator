==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Chain Reaction Pulsar]] EFFECT: Fires a special torpedo that bounces from target to target, gaining power with each impact. When fired, the CRP draws power from its initial target, then moves to a nearby target, drawing additional energy. The weapon continues to grow in power until it unleashes its entire accumulated force on a single, unfortunate final vessel.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
