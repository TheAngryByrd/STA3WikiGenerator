==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Survey]] EFFECT: Sends the scout to autmatically explore the area. Orders received - initiate automated survey of the designated area.
[[Tachyon Pulse]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked units. Emits a tachyon detection pulse from the scout vessel's deflector array to reveal cloaked ships up to 2500 units away.
[[Reveal Mines]] Countermeasure systems will seek to disrupt the cloaking field of enemy mines within 6000 units of the vessel.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
