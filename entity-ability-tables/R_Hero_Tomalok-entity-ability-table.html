==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Quantum Implosion]] EFFECT: Fires a pulse that significantly weakens the shields of surrounding enemies. This command uses the Terix's Quantum Singularity Core to generate a Level 5 Shockwave that damages and interferes with normal shield operations for a time.
[[Verteron Cascade]] EFFECT: Fires a pulse that significantly hampers surrounding enemy ships' ability to go to warp. This command emits a superluminal Verteron Cascade, which destabilizes subspace within the local region and significantly increases interplanetary travel time for hostile vessels travelling to and from the Terix's current position.
[[Formidable Foe]] Tales of Tomalak's impressive actions as the Commander of the Neutral Zone Fleet have reached far beyond the borders of the Empire, making opponents hesitant to target his vessel during engagements.
[[Commander Tomalak]] Tomalak, renowned Commander of the Neutral Zone Fleet, in command of his flagship, the D'deridex-class IRW Terix.
