==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Summon Gul Dukat]] Summons Gul Dukat, Leader of the Second Order and First Citizen of the Cardassian Union. Our enemies shall learn to fear his magisterial speeches!
[[Summon Weyoun]] Summons Weyoun, the current clone of an incomparably loyal Vorta Commander who reports directly to the Founders.
[[Summon Gul Madred]] Summons Gul Madred, a notoriously feared Union Guard interrogation officer. How many lights do you see?
[[Summon Thot Gor]] Summons Thot Gor, the chief liaison between the Breen Confederacy and the Dominion and a competent strategist.
[[Summon Gul Kentar]] Summons Gul Kentar, Leader of the Third Order, research scientist, and outspoken Cardassian isolationist.
