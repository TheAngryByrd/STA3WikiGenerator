==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Energy Drain Torpedo]] EFFECT: Fires a weapon that deals significant damage but also devastates the target's shield mitigation. The crew of this vessel are ordered to fire a deadly Energy Drain Torpedo, sapping the power of targeted ships, while also disabling their propulsion systems, weapons, and shields, making them easy prey for the rest of the fleet.
[[Boarding Team]] Sends a boarding party to attack an enemy frigate, cruiser or structure. If a target ship would be destroyed while boarded, the ship is instead healed for a portion of its shields and hull and brought under your control.
