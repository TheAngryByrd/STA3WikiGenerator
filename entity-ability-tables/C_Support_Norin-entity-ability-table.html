==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Anti-Cloak Scan]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked enemies. The crew of this vessel are ordered to activate a set of advanced anti-cloak scanning sensors, revealing cloaked mines and hostile vessels in the vicinity. Due to the power of these sensors, all systems will be disabled during the scan.
[[Target Sublight Engines]] The crew of this vessel are ordered to specifically target the enemy's sublight propulsion systems, impeding their ability to move and maneuver during combat.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
