==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Diplomatic Immunity]] Broadcasts of peace prevent the envoy from being targeted for a short time.
[[Establish Labour Camp]] EFFECT: Deploys a Cardassian Labour Camp in this sector that allows negotiation with Minor Factions or forces improved relations with major powers of the Alpha and Beta Quadrant. NOTE: You cannot deploy another embassy in the same system until your Envoy has left and returned to the system and the cooldown has expired.
[[Cultural Assistance]] The envoy transports teams to optimize production, decreasing upgrade costs and build times and improving relations.
[[Call For Ceasefire]] By projecting our honourable intentions throughout the system, diplomats are able to put a halt to planetary attacks.
