==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Engineering Teams]] Engineering teams on-board the spacedock begin repairs on nearby allied vessels.
[[Gravity Well]] Using the starbases reactor and deflector dishes a large artificial gravity well is formed affecting all vessels in the solar system.
[[Photon Barrage]] Unleashes a heavy-hitting barrage of photon torpedoes upon all enemy ships in range.
[[Corbomite Reflector]] EFFECT: Returns a portion of incoming weapons damage to the attacker. Generates a powerful Corbomite Shield, reflecting a portion of hostile weapons fire back toward the attacker's vessel.
