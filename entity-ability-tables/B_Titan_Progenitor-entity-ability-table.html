==Abilities==
Name Description
[[The 'Reaper' Beam]] EFFECT: Whatever the Progenitor fires this at goes away. Vessel designated for annihilation. Extermination device powering up.
[[Nanite Probes]] EFFECT: Deploys a small group of probes to support the Progenitor in combat. Release tactical nanite probes. Designate primary task: inhibit, assimilate, adapt.
[[Transwarp Hub]] EFFECT: Temporarily allows the Progenitor to function as a Transwarp Hub, allowing distant Borg ships with access to a Transwarp Gate to move directly to the Progenitor's location. Activate Transwarp portal. Vessels will now traverse the Transwarp Network.
[[Assimilation Cloud]] EFFECT: Sends out a pulse that disables enemy abilities and has a chance of assimilating nearby enemy ships. Release combat nanoprobes. The carbon-units will be assimilated.
