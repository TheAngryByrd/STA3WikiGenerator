==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Shuttlebay Expansion]] Expands all existing fighter bays to accommodate additional squadrons.
[[Rapid Launch Protocol]] EFFECT: Greatly increases strike craft launch rate for a time. Enables RLP mode, a contingency regulation that allows the launch of wings of fighter and bomber craft at a vastly expedited rate.
[[Fortress Mode]] EFFECT: Greatly increases firepower and durability at the expense of almost all mobility. Fortress Mode places the Typhon-class on lockdown, diverting a majority of power from the engines to heavily reinforce shields and weapons subsystems.
[[Point Defense System]] EFFECT: Reduces incoming damage. Advanced point defense systems installed on-board are activated, intercepting a significant portion of weapons fire.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
