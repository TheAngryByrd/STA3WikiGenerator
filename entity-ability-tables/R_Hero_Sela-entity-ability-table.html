==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Tal Shiar Network]] EFFECT: Allows Sela to scan distant gravity wells. This command utilizes Admiral Sela's wide-ranging influence to instruct Tal Shiar agents to infiltrate and monitor peripheral gravity wells located one step beyond the range of Imperial sensors.
[[Psionic Subverter]] EFFECT: Uses Psionic Mind Control to force affected ship's crew to lose their minds, disabling multiple ship systems on up to 15 targets. This command activates a secret psionic device stolen from the Terran Empire which disturbs the thoughts of enemy crews as they slowly go insane.
[[Tetryon Field Emitter]] Effect: A Passive field that lowers shield mitigation and shield restore rates, extremely powerful, installed on-board the N'Ventnar is the Tal Shiar's experimental Tetryon Field Emitter, a device which continuously floods the local region with bursts of tetryon particles, destabilizing the shield harmonics of all hostile vessels in range.
[[Admiral Sela]] Sela, two-blood Admiral of the Romulan Star Navy, commanding her flagship, the D'deridex-class IRW N'Ventnar.
