==Abilities==
Name Description
[[Scout]] EFFECT: Automatically scouts surrounding gravity wells. This command orders our scout vessel to swiftly and silently gather detailed information about nearby systems.
[[Tachyon Pulse]] EFFECT: Reveals surrounding cloaked units. This command activates a short-range tachyon pulse that reveals cloaked enemy vessels up to 2500 units away.
[[Detect Minefield]] This passive multiphasic scan detects hostile mines up to 6000 units away.
[[System Integrity]] As Systems are damaged, they will become less effective.
